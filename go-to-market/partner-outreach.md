# Partner Outreach — Attorneys, Public Adjusters, Insurance Brokers

## Target partners

**Tier 1 — Insurance attorneys (bad faith / coverage disputes)**
Best fit: solo/small firm attorneys who handle commercial property and bad faith claims.
They benefit: pre-qualified leads who understand the claim gap and are ready to litigate.
Model: referral fee ($200–$500 per converted lead) or revenue share.

**Tier 2 — Public adjusters**
Best fit: licensed public adjusters who work with commercial clients.
They benefit: pre-qualified leads who have already identified their gap.
Model: referral fee or white-label version of the tool.

**Tier 3 — Insurance brokers / agents**
Best fit: independent commercial insurance agents who want to add value for clients.
They benefit: differentiated service, client retention.
Model: free access for broker accounts + referral credit.

## Finding targets

**Insurance attorneys:**
- State bar association directories (search: "insurance," "bad faith," "commercial property")
- Avvo / Martindale / FindLaw — filter by practice area
- Google: "[state] commercial insurance bad faith attorney"

**Public adjusters:**
- National Association of Public Insurance Adjusters (NAPIA.com) — member directory
- Google: "[state] licensed public adjuster commercial property"

**Insurance brokers:**
- Independent Insurance Agents & Brokers of America (IIABA) directory
- Google: "[city] independent commercial insurance agent"
