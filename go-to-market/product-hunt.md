## Description (PH listing body — 300 words max)

Most small business owners accept their insurer's first settlement offer.

The average gap between that offer and the amount the policy actually supports: **$14,200**.

Shielded reads your commercial insurance policy — every page — and tells you exactly where you're being short-changed. Then it drafts the dispute letter for you.

**What it does:**
- Upload your commercial property policy PDF
- Claude AI reads every clause, identifies coverage gaps, and flags underpaid items
- You get a coverage score (0–100), a line-item risk report, and the exact policy sections that support a higher settlement
- One-click dispute letter draft — cites your policy by section number

**Who it's for:**
Restaurant owners, retailers, contractors, and any US small business with an active or recent insurance claim. Particularly useful if you've received a settlement offer and aren't sure if it's fair.

**What makes it different:**
We cover 106 commercial incident types across 1,514 US cities — with state-specific bad faith law, time limits, and claim rights built in. There are 162,000+ free claim guides at attorneyaitools.org/claims.

The AI analysis is powered by Claude, trained to read commercial policy language and identify the specific clauses that matter for your situation.

**Pricing:**
Free analysis (coverage score + risk flags). $49 one-time for the full dispute letter and settlement benchmarks. $29/month for ongoing policy monitoring.

No attorneys. No public adjusters. No $300/hr consultations.
